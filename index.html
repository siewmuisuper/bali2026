🌟 Fireflies Night Tour — Depart for Taro Village (Top Voted!)
The whole family drives to Taman Dukuh Agroforestry in Taro village (~30 min from Ubud). Arrive 5pm for a guided organic farm walk, then cook and enjoy a traditional Balinese dinner with locals. As darkness falls, walk the rice paddies to witness thousands of glowing fireflies — a magical welcome-to-Bali moment! Returns ~9pm. Knee-high boots provided.

Book via Firefly Tour Bali (fireflytourbali.com) — select Option B: Direct at Farm, 5:00 PM. Dinner is included in the tour.

Planning Notes
🌋
Mt Batur Sunrise
Scheduled as an optional pre-dawn departure on Day 2 night / Day 3 morning for 4 pax. Depart villa at 3am, summit by sunrise, back by 8am. Book with Bali Sunrise Trekking or similar licensed operator in advance.
🌅
Uluwatu + Kecak Dance
4 pax voted for this but with a 5:35pm departure on Day 5, it won't fit. Consider adding it as an evening activity on Day 4 instead of the BBQ — or make the BBQ an earlier dinner!
📸
Family Photographer
Only 1pt but worth flagging — consider a 2-hr villa session on Day 4 evening after BBQ, or sunset shoot at the beach. A small group sub-cost makes it very affordable.
🍳
Balinese Cooking Class
Also 1pt. Could replace one Day 3 afternoon activity if interest picks up. Many classes include a morning market visit — pairs beautifully with a relaxed Day 3 pace.
